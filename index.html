About Me

I received my B.S. and Master degrees in Information Engineering at Yangzhou University in 2009 and 2012, respectively. I received my Ph.D. degree in pattern recognition and intelligence system at Nanjing University of Science and Technology with Prof. Huaijiang Sun, in 2017. Currently, I am a Professor in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. I am also a member of the Machine Learning and Visual Perception (MLVP) with Prof. Yuhui Zheng.

I have been a visiting student working with Prof. Fatih Porikli at Australian National University (ANU) from Oct. 2015 to Oct. 2016. After 2018, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, worked with Prof. Weisi Lin.

Currently, our interests include machine learning, pattern recognition and computer vision (Person Re-identification, Object Detection, Image Super-resolution, Action Recognition & Semantic Segnmentaion)
